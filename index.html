This is just one example of an abandoned farm in the Westfjords region of Iceland. Despite what you might think this farm was right on the main highway.


This farm, not abandoned, was far removed from any major city or other settlement. Many of the farms we passed were pressed against the water like this, doubtless needing to take advantage of the resources and access offered by the sea.
